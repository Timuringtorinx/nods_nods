Cross-impact Matrix
helps analysts deal with complex problems when “everything is related to everything else.”

The Cross-Impact Matrix provides a structure for managing the complexity that makes most analysis so difficult. It requires that analysts clearly articulate all assumptions about the relationships among variables.
